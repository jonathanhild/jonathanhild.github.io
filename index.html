ABOUT ME

As a food scientist for the last 15 years, I've worked on problems in supply chain safety. Data has become critical in solving these issues and driven me to a career in data science.

I want to apply machine learning and NLP techniques to create tools that save time and provide people a richer world of information. Now, I'm in UW's Certificate of Data Science program to make this my next career.

I created this website to showcase my portfolio in data science and software development. Please, take a look at my blog to see what I've been working on, download my resume, or contact me to work together.
